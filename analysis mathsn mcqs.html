What Topics Are Actually Tested?

Frequency of each mathematics chapter across all 540 questions, sorted by dominance in the exam.

Trigonometry reigns supreme at 10.2%
With 55 out of 540 questions, Trigonometry is the single most tested chapter — particularly sin/cos identities, angle of elevation diagrams, and tan/cot relationships. Sets and Arithmetic Progressions tie for second at 9.6% each (52 questions each), making these three the "Big Three" that every student must master cold.

03 — REPEATED

Questions Asked Again & Again

These questions appear in multiple papers nearly verbatim — memorising their answers guarantees free marks.

The "angle between tangent and radius" question is a gift
Appearing in 14 out of 27 papers, this question always has the same answer: 90° (right angle). Similarly, the distance formula question using (sinθ, 0) and (0, cosθ) always equals 1 — and appears in 11 papers. These two alone are worth 2 marks if you just know the answers cold.
